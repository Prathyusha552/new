Benefits of Parental Involvement:

Research consistently highlights the numerous benefits of parental involvement in a child’s education. Here are some key advantages:

  • Improved Academic Performance: Studies show that children with involved parents tend to perform better in school, have higher test scores, and experience fewer disciplinary problems.
  • Enhanced Motivation: A child’s home environment plays a vital role in shaping their attitude towards learning. Parental interest and support can boost motivation and create a positive learning atmosphere.
  • Stronger Communication Skills: Parental involvement fosters open communication between parents and children. This translates into improved communication skills with teachers and peers at school.
  • Developed Social Skills: Engaging with your child’s school activities helps them feel connected and builds a sense of belonging. This fosters healthy social development and peer relationships.
  • Increased Confidence: When parents actively participate in their child’s education, it sends a powerful message of support and confidence.

How Sanskriti Encourages Parental Involvement:

  • Open Door Policy: Our teachers are highly approachable and encourage open communication with parents.

  • Parent Workshops and Seminars: We host workshops on various topics related to child development, learning strategies, and effective parenting practices.

Building a Strong Partnership:

Here are some additional ways parents can actively participate in their child’s CBSE school experience:

  • Create a Dedicated Learning Space: Establish a quiet and organized area at home to encourage your child’s studies.
  • Develop a Consistent Study Routine: Work with your child and teachers to create a regular study schedule.
  • Communicate Regularly with Teachers: Maintain open communication with teachers regarding your child’s progress.
  • Read Regularly with Your Child: Reading together promotes literacy skills and fosters a love of learning.
  • Support School Activities & Events: Attending school functions demonstrates your interest and support for your child’s academic and extracurricular involvement.
  • Be a Positive Role Model: Your own positive attitude towards learning can significantly influence your child’s approach to education.
